Reworked the defensive cooperation, colonial offices and foreign investment block principles.

-Defensive cooperation focus:
	·1 All members get +5% Unit defense, +1% Conscription rate. 
	   Cannot declare war on other block members.
	·2 All members get the previous tier's bonuses, +5% mill tech spread, -75% cost for swaying in plays.
	   The leader can impose their army model law on members.
	·3 All members improve conscription bonus to +2%, mill tech spread to +10%.
	   Members are forced to join defensive plays. Leader gets +5% additional unit defense.

-Colonial offices focus:
	·1 All members get +25% to colonial institution size change speed, -10% bureaucracy cost to
	   colonial institution and are dissalowed from removing colonial institution.
	   Leader can impose their colonial laws on members.
	·2 All members improve colonial institution size change speed to +40% and colonial institution 
	   bureaucracy cost to -20%. Member's colonial institution receives +0.1 to colony growth factor,
	   +4% throughput to plantations in unincorporated states and +4% throughput to rubber in
	   unincorporated states per level of colonial institution.
	·3 All members improve colonial institution size change speed to +50% and colonial institution 
	   bureaucracy cost to -50%. Member's colonial institution receives -10% to infamy generation against 
	   unrecognized nations, plantations in unincorporated states improve throughput to +6% per institution
	   level and rubber in unincorporated states improve throughput to +6% per level.
